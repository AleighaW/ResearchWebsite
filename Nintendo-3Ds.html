Nintendo 3DS

The Nintendo 3Ds is a foldable dual-screen handheld game console that was produced on February 26th, 2011. This console was able to be purchased until 2020. The Nintendo 3Ds was discontinued primarily because they were focusing mroe on their successful projects like the Switch.
