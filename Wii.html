Wii

The Wii is a home video game console released on November 19th, 2006. This console was so popular due to it's abililty to play games interactively while using a remote or nunchuks. The Wii has numerous amount of game options. Unfortunatley, no more games are made for the Wii today due to the upgraded consoles being more popular.
